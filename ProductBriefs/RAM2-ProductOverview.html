Please note that RAM2 has many options and features, that are not all covered in this manual. This manual serves as a user guide to help users who are unfamiliar with the RAM2 gain a brief understanding of how the product works, how to configure it for the first time, and how to use it. Please go through the steps detailed below to configure and use your RAM2 today. ## RAM2 Version 1.0 Features ### Factory management - Manage the shop floor: create operational layers to group data with similar operational characteristics. - Manage operational impacts: manage impacts values to operational layers to help in the risk prioritization. ### Asset management - Extract asset attributes: query MSB, as data collector, with values updated every 24 hours. - Business intelligence: Provide asset distribution views according to type and process. ### Alert management - Issue alerts on critical pre-defined changes: new asset discovery or asset disappear, changes asset in state, firmware version and IP, new vulnerability discovery. - Business intelligence: Provide asset distribution views according to type and process. ### Vulnerabilities management - Identify security vulnerabilities in assets in the environment: using a list of vulnerabilities compiled and updated by Otorio researchers. Currently more than 1,000 relevant ICS vulnerabilities are listed. ## Dashboard The dashboard shows the overall status of the shop floors in terms of assets, risk levels, and alerts. The Dashboard shows an interactive pie chart showing the risk level of each shop, and its production cells, in the factory. If there are no risks, the dashboard will indicate this as well. Production cells cards are displayed, for easy navigation to alert screens,to deal with the prioritization of the shop floor threats. Assets that have not yet been assigned to cells are shown in the “Unassigned assets” tab, with distributions according to alert types only. The risk level for an asset is calculated using a proprietary algorithm developed by Otorio, which considers the severities of vulnerabilities, as well as the number and types of alerts and assets in the network. ## Factory Management The first step in managing assets in the factory is grouping them into operational layers, for better mapping and visualization during later steps. You create “cards” (groups) for shops and production cells in their respective tabs under the Factory section. The user can manage basic attributes and operational impacts for each card. Risk level presents on each card if exist - The risk level is calculated by the unique Otorio’s algorithm, which considers the severities of vulnerabilities, as well as the number and types of alerts, assets in the network and managed operational impacts on production cells or assets. Assets are grouped and assigned to cells, and cells assigned to shopa. Assets are configured and managed in the Assets tab under the Factory section. ## Assets Management Assets and their attributes are automatically collected from external collectors (such as the MSB). Details such as IP and MAC addresses, types, vendor, firmware and hardware versions, family, and more, are collected and displayed for each asset. The following customized characteristics and attributions are assigned to assets: - Device Name - Description - Type (controller, engineering stations, HMIs, servers, controllers, I/Os, other ICS devices and networking equipment) - Location - Production Cell - Impact Levels (based on several factors: financial, safety productivity, operational, reputation, and regulator) The asset inventory is synchronized with the Otorio Threat Intelligence Module that provides knowledge about known and unknown vulnerabilities that are discovered in assets. The assets can be filtered based on various categories and characteristics. ## Investigate - Alerts The Alerts section shows alerts that have been generated by the system. RAM2 automatically generates the following types of alerts: - New Asset Discovered - Asset not seen (configurable interval) - New Vulnerability Discovered (known and unknown vulnerability on an asset with sync to Otorio’s Threat Intelligence Module*) - Firmware Version Changed - IP Address Changed - State Changed In order to remediate the risks and handle the alerts, they must go through an Acknowledgement process. The upcoming versions of RAM2 will automatically provide mitigation recommendation based on the alerts. As with the asset management, the alerts can also be filtered based on various categories and characteristics. ### Disabling alerts with certain CVE This feature helps minimize the number of alerts. The user can decrease the number of alerts being generated by the system by disabling alerts with any chosen CVE. *Otorio’s Threat Intelligence Module integrated in RAM2, is constantly updated and managed by Otorio’s researchers. The current version contains over 1,000 relevant ICS vulnerabilities.
